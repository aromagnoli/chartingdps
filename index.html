Charting DPS

Here you can see charts about every dps class and how good they are performing for the current content.
The data used here was gathered from SimulationCraft.
Remember: what do you see here are simulations and not necessarily match ingame numbers.

What you can do here?

  • Zoom in and out by clicking in the chart and dragging the mouse's pointer around.
  • Export a screenshot at the top right menu of each chart.
  • Click on the legends to hide or show specific lines.
